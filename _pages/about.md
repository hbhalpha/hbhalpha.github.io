Short Bio
---   

My name is Bohan Hou (侯博涵), and I am currently an incoming PhD student at Nanyang Technological University (NTU). I received my undergraduate education at [Shandong University (山东大学)](https://www.sdu.edu.cn) with a first honor degree, where I studied computer science at [Taishan College (泰山学堂，first-honor program)](https://www.tsxt.sdu.edu.cn) under the guidance of Associate Professor [Xuemeng Song (宋雪萌)](https://xuemengsong.github.io/). I have also been a research assistant in the [Intelligent Media Research Center (iLearn)](https://ilearn.qd.sdu.edu.cn), led by Professor [Liqiang Nie (聂礼强)](https://liqiangnie.github.io). My research interests include **multimodal models**, **information retrieval**, **embodied intelligence**, deep learning, and related areas.
